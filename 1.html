Alphabets

Kana word divides two type:
Hirakana looks like more soft and round than Katakana. Hirakana had beed used by women, not man. But now it is used not only women but men. Hirakana is equivalent at lower English alphabet.(actually, it is quite diffrent. but at first time to learn Japanese, it is easy to understand)

Katakana looks like more sharp and square, like Chinease. Katakana had been used by men, not women. But like Hirakana, Katakana is also used women and men both. Katakana is generally used expressing strong word or foreign word.
